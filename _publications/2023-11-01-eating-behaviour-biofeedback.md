Conference abstract and oral presentation at the TIMIC Third International Motor Impairment Conference (Sydney, November 2023). The work presents a real-time video analysis pipeline using object and pose tracking to quantify scooping and food-transfer movements, supporting eating-behaviour biofeedback for children with cerebral palsy.
